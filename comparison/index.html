⚠️ Warning: Honey's Cookie Hijacking

How it works: You click an affiliate link → Honey detects checkout → Replaces the cookie → Takes the commission → Original blogger gets $0 → You still pay full price.

Why it's bad: Honey has 20M users stealing commissions from content creators who helped you find the product. It's legal, but it's stealing. That's why they have a 1.7★ Trustpilot rating (53 verified complaints).

Our promise: We can't hijack cookies (we don't have permission in manifest.json). Verify it yourself in the source code.
